Elite Ballers Connect - Site Plan

Site Name

Elite Ballers Connect is a platform dedicated to connecting basketball players, coaches, and enthusiasts. The name reflects the elite nature of competitive basketball and the community-driven aspect of the site.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a hub for basketball players to network, find training resources, view game strategies, attian scholarships ,and connect with teams and leagues.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find basketball training tips and drills?
  • How do I connect with local teams and leagues?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Blue (#0A3D91) - Used for headers and accents.
Secondary Color: Orange (#F28C28) - Used for buttons and highlights.
Background: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) - Used for page background.

Typography

Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif
Body Text: Roboto, sans-serif
