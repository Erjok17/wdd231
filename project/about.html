Who We Are

Elite Ballers Connect is a platform that uses tech and guidance to help players, coaches, schools, and professional teams to connect with each other so that they maximize each other's values in the world of basketball. Our experienced recruiters give personalized consulting to players, coaches, schools, and professional teams, while our online program platform empowers them to connect with each other.

Known as a "must-have" recruiting platform, with over 1,000 individuals in the basketball world using it to find their needs in basketball. Since 2024, Elite Ballers Connect has provided connections among players and coaches around the world, recruiting advice, and an extensive set of tools and services that help on their mission to play their favorite sport, Basketball.
